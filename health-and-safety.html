Photosensitivity and Seizure Warning

A very small percentage of people may experience a seizure when exposed to certain visual images, including flashing lights or patterns. BEST
PRACTICES

  • Take Breaks: We recommend taking a 15-minute break for every hour of play.
  • Lighting: Play in a well-lit room.
  • Listen to your body: If you feel tired, dizzy, or have strained eyes, stop playing immediately.
  • Mental Wellbeing: Gaming should be fun, not stressful. If you find yourself becoming frustrated, it's time to step away.
