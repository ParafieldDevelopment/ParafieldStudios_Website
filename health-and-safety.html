Play Smart, Stay Safe.

At Parafield Studios, we want you to enjoy our games for years to come. That starts with taking care of yourself. Please read this information carefully before using our software.

02 // MEDICAL_PROTOCOL
Photosensitivity and Seizure Warning

A very small percentage of people may experience a seizure when exposed to certain visual images, including flashing lights or patterns that may appear in video games. Even people who have no history of seizures or epilepsy may have an undiagnosed condition that can cause these "photosensitive epileptic seizures" while watching video games.

General Health Recommendations

  • Take Breaks: We recommend taking a 15-minute break for every hour of play.
  • Lighting: Play in a well-lit room.
  • Listen to your body: If you feel tired, dizzy, or have strained eyes, stop playing immediately.

Mental Wellbeing

Gaming should be fun, not stressful. If you find yourself becoming frustrated, it's time to step away.
