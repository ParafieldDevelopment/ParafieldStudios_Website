POLICY

We are gonna make this simple…

“We DO NOT want your data. Keep that shit to yourselves.”

That telemetry data you expect

At Parafield Studios (and our subsidiaries like REDTOPS), we believe your personal life is none of our business. We aren't data brokers, and we aren't interested in your digital footprint.

What we DO collect however is Hardware Telemetry data.

Why we collect it: To make sure our products and games actually run on your machine no matter how shitty it might be. We use this data to push updates and fix bugs. That is it.

This includes:

  • GPU/CPU models
  • OS Version
  • Crash logs and stability metrics

What we DON'T see:

  • Your name
  • Your email
  • Your location
  • OR what you had for breakfast.

If you however do not want any telemerty data to be collected INCLUDING hardware data. You may disable them via the settings menu from any of our games or products.
